About Me

Hi! I'm Muhammad Arifin, a graduate of SMK Negeri 2 Magelang majoring in Software and Game Development (Software Engineering). I am passionate about creating digital solutions that are both functional and visually appealing. For me, coding is not just about writing lines of code, but an art of solving problems.

Throughout my journey in web development, I have built various projects that have strengthened my full-stack development skills. One of my key achievements is developing EventKu, an integrated event management platform that includes a ticketing system and a complex admin dashboard.

I highly value UI/UX consistency and application performance. With expertise in technologies such as PHP (Laravel), Tailwind CSS, and automated testing using Cypress, I am committed to delivering products that not only run smoothly but also provide a memorable user experience.
